Pizza Dough

Description

This delicious thin crust pizza dough recipe is so low maintenance, you can throw it together in just 15 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar and water
  • Flour
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water.
  2. Make the dough.
  3. Knead the dough.
  4. Roll dough into circle.
  5. Bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit.
